### Slide 5 ŌĆö Churns & At-Risk Customers

**Purpose:** See who churned this period, who is at risk, and whether churn is trending up
or down over time.

**Layout (top to bottom):**
1. `summary-grid` ŌĆö 4 KPI cards: churned count (2), at-risk count (4), MRR at risk ($5,250),
   MRR churned ($2,000)
2. **Churn trend chart** (full-width card, max-height 180px) ŌĆö bar chart, red/orange,
   shows churn count per period
3. `row` ŌĆö two side-by-side tables: Churned this period | At-risk accounts

**Churn trend chart details:**
- Chart type: bar
- Fill: `rgba(248,113,113,0.75)`, border: `#F87171`
- Y-axis: integer, beginAtZero, stepSize 1
- X-axis: same PERIODS labels
- Legend: hidden
- Canvas height: max 180px (quick glance, not main focus)
- Canvas id: `chart-churn-trend`
